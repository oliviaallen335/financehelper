Annuity Streams (Annuities)
Annuities are a level stream of cash flows at regular intervals with a finite maturity.
- Constant periodic cash flow (in or out)
- Can begin at the start of each period (annuity due) as is true of rent and insurance payments or at the end of each period, (ordinary annuity) as in the case of mortgage and loan payments.
- Foundation of nearly all consumer and business lending

Commercial Lending
Amortization: repaying a loan or debt via an annuity cash flow
- A constant periodic payment pays off the interest and part of the principal balance each period
- Last payment services any remaining interest and principal balance
- For lender, no uncertainty in cash flow from loan; for consumer, a regular scheduled payment (best terms for the consumer!)
- The backbone of nearly all consumer and commercial loan products (mortgages, auto loans, credit cards, etc.)
